About Us!

This is a project class for SDEV 220 by Group F and it is a Food Ordering System that is online resembling those of other popular websites such as Doordash or any Fast Food web application. You can browse our menu, place an order, and have food delivered to your doorstep (Conceptually, of course). This web application was built by Neftali Martinez, Maika Innocent and Shia Cudjoe. Both Neftali Martinez, and Maika Innocent were involved in the programming of the web app. While Shia Cudjoe designed the web design elements of the page.
